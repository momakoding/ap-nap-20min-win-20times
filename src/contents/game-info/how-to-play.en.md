## Objective

You are **Tonald Dump**, attending the APEC Summit with exactly **20 minutes of precious rest** (2 real-world minutes).

Before the countdown hits zero, keep your Health and Diplomatic Reputation as high as possible while racking up your **Win Counter**. Ultimate hidden achievement: **Win 20 times in 20 minutes!**

---

## Four Core Stats

| Stat | Starting Value | Cap | Consequence at Limit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ❤️ Health | 40 | 100 | Instant "Cardiac Arrest" ending |
| 🌐 Diplomacy | 60 | 100 | Instant "Kicked from Group Chat" ending |
| 😤 Stress | 30 | 100 | Reaching 100 triggers "Phone Smash" ending |
| 🏆 Win Counter | 0 | Unlimited | More wins = more glorious final rating |

> ⚠️ When Stress exceeds 70, it passively drains Health each second. Keep it in check!

---

## Basic Gameplay

**Main Screen**: Click Rest Action buttons to spend game time and recover Health.

**Interrupt Events**: Every ~15 seconds an urgent event pops up. You have **8 seconds** to decide:

- **Option A (Answer)**: Costs real time, usually boosts Diplomacy and adds a win
- **Option B (Hang Up)**: No time cost, but may drop Diplomacy or spike Stress
- **Option C (Defer)**: No time cost, small stat penalty, some events re-queue

> ⚠️ If you run out the timer, Option C is automatically chosen

## Tips

- When Stress climbs past 70, interrupts come **much faster** — prioritize keeping stress down
- Health critical? Go for **McDonald's Feast** or **Mystery Pills** for the biggest gains
- Chasing the 20-win achievement? Choose **Option A** as much as possible
- Short actions like **Fix the Hairdo** or **Diet Coke** are perfect for squeezing in between interrupts
